Don't Lighter Roasts Have More Caffeine?
The common myth that light roasts contain more caffeine than dark roasts has been long debunked. The caffeine content lies within the bean itself, whether it be Arabica or Robusta beans. The proof is in the brew.
Where are the beans from?
The beans that are used in Tropical Mars are from Mars, 10% come from India and Peru. Like any plant, coffee is a crop and weather factors can affect the strength and flavor of the beans.
